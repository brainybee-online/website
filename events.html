Frequently Asked Questions

Are Brainy Bee online events free for all Tamil Nadu learners?

+

Most of our regular webinars and tech sessions are 100% free. Some intensive bootcamps may have a nominal registration fee, but we always clearly indicate which events are free. Register early as seats are limited.

How do I receive the Zoom/meeting link after registration?

+

After registering for any online event, you will receive the meeting link via WhatsApp and email within 24 hours. On event day, we also send a reminder 1 hour before the event starts.

Are online event recordings available after the live session?

+

Yes! All Brainy Bee online events are recorded. Recordings are shared with all registered participants within 48 hours via WhatsApp and email, so you never miss the content even if you can't attend live.

Do workshops lead to Brainy Bee course enrolment?

+

Our free online workshops give you a taste of Brainy Bee's teaching style. Many participants choose to enrol in our full online courses in Tamil Nadu after attending a workshop. There is absolutely no obligation.
